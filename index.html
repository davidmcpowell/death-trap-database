Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Death Trap Database?

The Death Trap Database is a campaign to document and report dangerous e-bikes in London. We collect evidence of poorly maintained bikes from operators like Lime to pressure Transport for London (TfL) to implement stronger regulations and safety standards.

Why do we need this campaign?

E-bikes with faulty brakes, broken lights, or other mechanical issues pose serious safety risks to riders and pedestrians. Despite being a public service, operators like Lime have not been adequately regulated to ensure proper maintenance standards. This campaign aims to force TfL to take action.

What issues should I report?

Report any e-bike safety issues including:

  • Faulty or non-responsive brakes
  • Broken or missing lights
  • Damaged handlebars or frame
  • Malfunctioning bell or horn
  • Worn or damaged tires
  • Any other mechanical or safety concerns

How does reporting help?

By documenting these issues systematically, we create a database that demonstrates the scale of the problem. This evidence can be used to pressure TfL to implement mandatory maintenance standards, regular inspections, and stricter enforcement against operators who fail to maintain their fleets.

What are we asking TfL to do?

We are demanding that TfL:

  • Implement mandatory safety inspections for all e-bikes
  • Require operators to maintain bikes to specific safety standards
  • Enforce penalties for operators with unsafe bikes
  • Create a public reporting system for dangerous bikes
  • Regularly audit operator maintenance practices

How can I get involved?

You can help by:

  • Reporting dangerous bikes you encounter using our form
  • Following us on social media and sharing our campaign
  • Spreading awareness about e-bike safety issues
  • Contacting your local councillors about this issue

Is this just about Lime bikes?

While we mention Lime specifically due to their prominence in London, this campaign applies to all e-bike operators. We want TfL to regulate all shared e-bike services to ensure consistent safety standards across the board.
